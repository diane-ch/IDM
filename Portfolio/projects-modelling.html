Modelling

3D-scene modelling

Using the software 3DS Max, I modelled a 3D-scene inspired by the game Cluedo. I modelled from scratch the five following objects: magnifying glass, candlestick, gun, fireplace and pooltable. For the rendering, I used texture maps and bump maps found on the websites PolyHaven and Textures.com.
